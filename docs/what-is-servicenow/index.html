What is ServiceNow?

ServiceNow is the name of both a software system and the company that makes it. ServiceNow is focused on business process automation, providing software tools to augment or fully automate all manner of processes from tech support to HR. The features of the platform are grouped into 14 capabilities in 4 categories.

Within ISD, we currently use 2 of the capabilities – IT Service Management and IT Business Management. Our instance of ServiceNow is called My Ministry Service Desk (MMSD) and is accessed via: https://bcsocialsector.service-now.com – note, only users with valid social sector IDIRs may access the system.

Most people accessing the system will be using the portal. This is the “front door” of the system and allows people to submit service requests, consume knowledge management articles and other tasks. The back-end of the system is how ISD staff and others do their work and fulfill tasks.
