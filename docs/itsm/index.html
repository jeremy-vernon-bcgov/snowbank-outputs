IT Service Management (ITSM)

The IT Service Management component of ServiceNow is made of four components that work in concert to enable our Service Management Operations (SMO) team. The ServiceNow ITSM system is an implementation of a standard called ITIL (IT Information Library).

The ITSM “stack” manages four things: changes, incidents, problems and requests.

  • Change Management IT infrastructure must be continuously updated, extended and renewed. Change management handles the coordination of work to ensure changes are made quickly and safely.

  • Incident Management Systems can go offline or malfunction. When something goes wrong, incident management helps notify people and coordinate the response.

  • Problem Management Incidents have causes. Addressing those causes is problem management. This system coordinates that work.

  • Request Management Employees need IDIRs, laptops, software licenses and many other things. The request management system takes in requests and helps coordinate fulfillment.

Put together, these four components form a unified service management system.
